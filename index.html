Who We Are

Men in New Directions (MIND MSU) is a student-led organization at Michigan State University dedicated to transforming the culture of men’s mental health. We provide a supportive, substance-free community where Spartans can discover what true strength means: not silence or toughness, but clarity of mind, emotional intelligence, and resilience.

Our club redefines masculinity by promoting wellness, accountability, and purpose-driven living. We believe every student has the capacity to grow stronger when supported by community. While our primary focus is men’s mental health, we welcome all Spartans who share our mission of living with clarity, resilience, and intention.
